Who We Are

We are a group of students in the New Jersey area who have all attended college in the midst of the Coronavirus pandemic. We created this website to assist the front liners of COVID-19 while maintaining social-distancing or social-isolation. Here, you can find local restaurants in your area that offer contactless delivery, local socially-distant or virtual events, and local hospitals accepting Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) donations. Thank you for your support in these troubling times and please stay safe.
